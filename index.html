üìú League Rules & Format

  • Match Format: All matches are Best of 7 legs (First to 4). If a player reaches 4 legs, the match ends immediately.
  • Points System:
    • 3 Points for the overall Match Win.
    • 1 Point for every single leg won (e.g., a 4-2 win earns the winner 7 points and the loser 2 points).
  • Season Structure: Seasons last 8 weeks. Fixtures are generated weekly. You have a 14-day window from the fixture date to complete your game.
  • Technical Requirements: Cameras must be on and clear at all times during play. Discord or your preferred video calling app is required.
  • Submission & Evidence: All results must be submitted via the "Submit Score" page.
    • A screenshot of the final score is mandatory for every match.
    • 180s and High Checkouts (100+) must be evidenced via a photo of the board to be recorded on the season leaderboards.
  • Promotion & Relegation: At the end of the 8-week season, the top 2 players in each league move up, and the bottom 2 players move down.
